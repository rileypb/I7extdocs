Bit Ops by Zed Lopez

Adds phrases for basic logic operations:

- bit-not - bit-or - bit-and - bit-xor/xor

unsigned shift left: - unsigned/logical shifted/shift left by/of/--

unsigned shift right: - unsigned/logical shift/shifted right by/of/--

signed shift right: - signed/arithmetic/-- shift/shifted right by/of/--
